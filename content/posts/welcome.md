Welcome to **200word.org** - a simple, markdown-based blog focused on sharing thoughts in approximately 200 words.

This blog is built with Hugo and automatically published to GitHub Pages. Each post is written in Markdown and kept concise.

## Why 200 Words?

Brevity is the soul of wit. By limiting posts to around 200 words, we focus on clarity and essential ideas without unnecessary elaboration.

## What to Expect

- Short, focused posts
- No comments or interactions
- Pure markdown content
- Simple, clean design

Thanks for visiting!
